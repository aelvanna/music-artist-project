Who are GOT7?

GOT7 is a South Korean boy band formed by JYP Entertainment. The group is composed of seven members:

Name Birthday
Jay B (Lim Jaebeom) January 6 1994
Mark Tuan September 1993
Jackson Wang 28 March 1994
Jinyoung Park 22 September 1994
Youngjae Choi 17 September 1996
BamBam (Kunpimook Bhuwakul) May 2 1997
Yugyeom Kim 17 November 1997

About GOT7

GOT7 debuted on January 16 2014 and have been nominated as Top Social Artist at Billboard Music Awards and charted #1 on iTunes in 42 differnt countries. This has allowed GOT7 to be identified as global artists with fans all over the world. Furthermore Mark, Jackson and BamBam are multilingual and are Taiwanese-American, Chinese and Thai respectively adding to the group's identity as global artists.

My Favourite Songs by GOT7

Encore

Encore is a song written and composed by GOT7's Jinyoung. It is their first release under Warner Music Korea, and is a message to the fans, reassuring them that they will be GOT7 forever and will always come back to sing, hence the name "Encore".

Release

February 20 2021

Lyrics

"We will sing for you

We will sing for you

Encore! Encore!

We will sing for you

For the rest of the days too

Encore! Encore!"

See full lyrics

NANANA

NANANA is the title track of GOT7's self-titled EP "GOT7". GOT7's leader JAY B, composed the song as a gift to the fans and best represents GOT7's sound. He has expressed that it contains the hope that they can make their fans happy and that their songs can be healing during difficult and tiring daily life.

Release

May 23 2022

Lyrics

"By your side, na-na-na

Someone who wants to make you laugh, na-na-na

It feels good, na-na-na

I wonder about everything "

See full lyrics

You Are

You Are is the title track of GOT7's seventh mini album "7 for 7" GOT7's leader Jay B, contributed to the composition of the song under his pseudonym "Defsoul". It is a song that expresses gratitude towards a loved one for their support by comparing them to the sky that one would look at when exhausted to gain the motivation to move on.

Release

October 10 2017

Lyrics

"It's a beautiful sky

The sky is clear, like you

There is not a cloud, because of you

The world feels different to me"
