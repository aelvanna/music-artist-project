Who are GOT7?

Global Artists

GOT7 is a South Korean boy band formed by JYP Entertainment. The group is composed of seven members: Jay B, Mark, Jackson, Jinyoung, Youngjae, BamBam, and Yugyeom. GOT7 debuted on January 16 2014 and have been nominated as Top Social Artist at Billboard Music Awards and charted #1 on iTunes in 42 differnt countries. This has allowed GOT7 to be identified as global artists with fans all over the world. Furthermore Mark, Jackson and BamBam are multilingual and are Taiwanese-American, Chinese and Thai respectively adding to the group's identity as global artists.

On January 2021, their contract with JYP Entertainment expired and all members left JYP entertainment and signed with different agencies to manage their solo careers, while choosing to also remain as GOT7 by partnering with Warner Music Korea. Despite being under Warner Music Korea, all copyright for their group name and previously released music were transferred to the members themselves.

One month later on February 2021, GOT7 released their Single "Encore", which is dedicated to the fans, and represents their promise to continue on as GOT7 forever. After focusing on solo activities for around a year, GOT7 released their self-titled EP "GOT7" on May 23 2022. With this release, GOT7 launched new social media accounts and a new logo (seen below) for the group accompanied with the phrase "GOT7 is our name". They also held their Homecoming 2022 Fancon" at SK Olympic Handball Gymnasium on May 21 - May 22 2022.

NANANA

NANANA is the title track of GOT7's self-titled EP "GOT7". GOT7's leader JAY B, composed the song as a gift to the fans and best represents GOT7's sound. He has expressed that it contains the hope that they can make their fans happy and that their songs can be healing during difficult and tiring daily life.

You Are

You Are is the title track of GOT7's seventh mini album "7 for 7" GOT7's leader Jay B, contributed to the composition of the song under his pseudonym "Defsoul".
